SEVENTH COLLECTION

CALIFORNIA WINTER 2020

FEAR OF GOD ATHLETICS

Seven: the number of completeness and perfection. The offering: an immovable narrative, rooted in perpetuity and unbound by time.

Two years in the making – the seventh collection rooted in easy elegance, our history and your reality; introduces suiting, knits and accessories to our audience for the first time.
Traditional tailoring is transformed, adapted and updated in the form of transitional and trans-seasonal fits. Crossing disciplines and re-envisioning formalwear through the lens of modern functionality and performance needs, to continually challenge received notions of what contemporary fashion is today. Juxtaposing time-honored and contemporary fabrics with our signature considered yet understated finishes to sit seamlessly alongside our staple denims, cottons, canvases, leathers and suedes in enduring silhouettes for yesterday, today and tomorrow.
Wool and cashmere knits present an elegant evolution of our classic sweatshirts and hoodies, while bags and belts, devoid of the distraction of hardware, are pared down to the purest version of themselves meeting at the convergence of fashion and function. The Seventh Collection also marks the unveiling of a new iteration of our FG logo in a military-inspired stencil font and a tribute to America’s favorite pastime, baseball, with a new lineup of stylized graphics celebrating The Negro Leagues.
Inspired by our own journey navigating the path from nowness to eternality, The Seventh Collection marks our coming of age.

A modern legacy, with an eternal perspective.

Presenting the Fear of God Tailoring Collection. Handmade in Italy, home to the most renowned ateliers in the world. Crafted in a mix of classic luxury and innovative fabrics sourced from the most reputable mills in Italy and England and expressed through the lens of modernity, functionality and versatility to merge effortlessly into our style vernacular and your wardrobe.

Distilling the fundamentals of style and function.

Introducing Fear of God Accessories: fundamental necessities including bags, belts, and wallets are pared back and re-elevated thru our perspective of tomorrow. Made in Italy, hand-stitched and finished, and presented in waxed suede, Nappa calf leather and technical nylon each piece is the embodiment of our functional and effortless aesthetic and a conclusive statement of the collection.
